SKA AlarmHandler¶

SKAAlarmHandler

A generic base device for Alarms for SKA. It exposes SKA alarms and SKA alerts as TANGO attributes. SKA Alarms and SKA/Element Alerts are rules-based configurable conditions that can be defined over multiple attribute values and quality factors, and are separate from the “built-in” TANGO attribute alarms.
